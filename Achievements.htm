ACM - ICPC

ACM-ICPC stands for Association for Computing Machinery - International Collegiate Programming Contest .The ACM ICPC is considered as the "Olympics of Programming Competitions". It is quite simply, the oldest, largest, and most prestigious programming contest in the world.

It is one of the Most sort after contests for all the Competitve coding geeks out there.
For continously two years, 2-3 teams from our college have been actively participating in this contest and have secured a best rank of 25 at the Regionals state.

Smart India Hackathon

Smart India Hackathon 2019 is a nationwide initiative to provide students a platform to solve some of pressing problems we face in our daily lives, and thus inculcate a culture of product innovation and a mindset of problem solving. SIH is one of the most sort after hackathons held in India as participants from all over the India take upon themselves to solve the daily problems faced by the people using Software/hardware or both.

In the Academic year 2018-2019, 12 students of our college took part in this hackathon and made our college proud.

Processing Foundation Fellowships of New York University

The Processing Foundation Fellowships support artists, coders, and collectives in visionary projects that conceive a new direction for what Processing as a software and a community can do. The main mission is to promote software literacy within the visual arts, and visual literacy within technology-related fields.

Two of our students were part of this program and made their name and the institute's name at the International level.
Mr. Shaharyar Shamshi, Batch 2015-2019, CSE Department
Miss. Nancy Chauhan, Batch 2015-2019, ECE Department

Research Papers

One of our Student, Miss. Nancy Chauhan has also driven her hands in the Field of Research. One of her Research paper namely:
"A survey on the Application of Machine Learning in Wireless Sensor Networks", got accepted and published at International Conference on Machine Learning, Image Processing, Network Security and Data Sciences, MIND-2019.

Another Research Paper of hers, namely:
"Suprathreshold Stochastic Resonance for Gamma Noise With Watermarking Application" has been accepted for a oral presentation at the 25th International Conference on Noise and Fluctuations(ICNF 2019), to be held at Neuchatel, Switzerland.
